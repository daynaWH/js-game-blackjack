How To Play

  • This is a simplified version of blackjack where the player plays a single round against the dealer.
  • Goal of the Game
    • To win the game, the player needs to get closer to the value of 21 than the dealer without going over 21.
  • Game Rules
    • The player and the dealer are initially dealt two cards.
    • After the initial deal, the player has the option to stand or hit for another card.
    • Once the player stands or goes over 21, the dealer plays its cards.
  • How Points are Calculated:
    • Aces are worth either 1 or 11. If the total exceeds 21, it's automatically counted as 1.
    • Kings, Queens and Jacks are worth 10.
    • The number cards (2-10) are worth the value of their number.
